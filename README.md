# 🚀 LoyalLens

## Know who loves your brand and who loves your discounts.

LoyalLens is a Customer Intelligence Platform designed to help businesses understand customer behavior beyond revenue numbers. It identifies genuinely loyal customers, discount-driven buyers, at-risk customers, and high-value customer segments to support smarter marketing and retention strategies.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Most businesses can measure sales and revenue, but they often struggle to answer critical questions:

* Which customers are genuinely loyal?
* Which customers only purchase because of discounts?
* Which customers are likely to stop buying?
* How is customer behavior changing over time?
* Which customers deserve targeted retention campaigns?

Without these insights, businesses risk spending marketing budgets inefficiently and losing valuable customers.

---

## 💡 Solution

LoyalLens transforms raw customer transaction data into actionable business intelligence.

By analyzing customer purchase behavior, spending patterns, recency, and discount dependency, LoyalLens helps businesses make data-driven decisions that improve retention and profitability.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 📂 Flexible CSV Upload

Upload customer transaction data from any business system.

### 🔄 Smart Column Mapping

Map uploaded columns to LoyalLens fields without requiring a fixed format.

### 📊 Data Quality Assessment

Detect missing or incomplete fields and provide a Data Quality Report.

### 🧮 Loyalty Score Calculation

Evaluate customers using configurable business-defined scoring models.

### 🎯 Promo Dependency Analysis

Identify customers who primarily purchase because of discounts.

### 👥 Customer Segmentation

Automatically classify customers into meaningful business segments.

### 📈 Executive Dashboard

View customer distribution, trends, and business insights.

### 📥 Downloadable Reports

Export segmented customer lists for marketing and retention campaigns.

### 🔄 Customer Movement Tracking

Track how customers move between segments across multiple months.

---

## 👥 Customer Segments

### 🟢 Loyal Customers

High engagement, frequent purchases, and low discount dependency.

### 🟡 Potential Loyalists

Promising customers who show strong growth potential.

### 🟠 At-Risk Customers

Customers whose activity is declining and require retention efforts.

### 🔴 Discount-Driven Customers

Customers who primarily purchase when promotions are available.

### 🟣 High-Value Discount Dependent Customers

High-spending customers who generate significant revenue but rely heavily on discounts.

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. Upload customer transaction data.
2. Map dataset columns.
3. Validate data quality.
4. Calculate customer metrics and scores.
5. Segment customers automatically.
6. Generate recommendations and reports.
7. Track customer movement over time.
